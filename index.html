About Our Foundation

The Megan Foundation was established in honor of our daughter, Megan, who lost her hearing at the age of four.

As the largest provider of hoodies supporting the deaf community in Kampala, the foundation aims to raise awareness and foster inclusivity. Through our initiatives, we not only provide essential resources but also create a sense of belonging for individuals with hearing impairments, celebrating their strength and resilience.
